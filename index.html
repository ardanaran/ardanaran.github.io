ABOUT ICARELI CONFERENCE

Food security and adequacy become an important issue after the pandemic situation is over. The adequacy of food is influenced by the global political situation, so many observers predict that 2023 is a tough year to go through. Food security and food safety are basic needs for people, worldwide. Food of animal origin is one of the main sources of protein which is fundament, not only mandatory to be enough but also requires safety guarantees for consumers.
